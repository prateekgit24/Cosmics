About Me

Hello! I am a passionate engineering student and the creator behind Cosmics. Exploring the realms of technology and constantly learning has always been a core part of who I am.

My Background

Navigating through an engineering curriculum, I've developed a profound appreciation for solving complex problems through code. My journey involves breaking things, figuring out why they broke, and then building them better. This iterative process has honed my software engineering skills, driving me toward creating robust and beautifully designed tools.

The Purpose

Working on projects like FocusGuard and Anya represents my deeper mission: to develop applications that genuinely improve people's digital and real-life experiences. Whether it's helping students focus better in a world filled with distractions or building intelligent AI companions that adapt to a user's needs, my projects aim to connect technology with meaningful utility.

Thank you for stopping by and exploring my work. I hope you find as much value in using these tools as I found in building them.
